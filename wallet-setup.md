# Basic Wallet Setup

## Initial Setup

1. Connect your Trezor device
2. Launch Trezor Suite
3. Follow the device initialization wizard

## Creating a New Wallet

1. Choose "Create new wallet"
2. Write down your recovery seed
3. Verify your recovery seed
4. Set up PIN protection
